What is

SWAG

SWAG is designed with the aim of breaking the monotony of traditional learning methods, SWAG transforms studying into an exciting adventure.

SW: SWING SWAG swings learning into an exciting and dynamic experience, adding a new dimension to traditional education methods.

A: ACADEMIC SWAG focuses on academic excellence, providing students with opportunities to excel in various subjects and skills.

G: GAME SWAG turns learning into a game, making it fun, interactive, and engaging for students of all ages

SWAG Competition Details

The SWAG competition invites primary and junior secondary school students from Ekiti State to engage in a dynamic and exciting academic challenge. With a focus on promoting excellence across various subjects and skills, SWAG competitions offer a platform for students to showcase their knowledge and abilities in a fun and interactive environment.

Registration Opens for all states: April 29th - May 24th, 2024

Pre-Qualifying Stage: May 27th - June 1st, 2024

Competition Runs:

  • Ekiti (June 3rd - 8th of June)
  • Kwara (June 11th - 15th of June)
  • Ondo (17th of June - 22nd of June)
  • Ogun (June 19th - 26th of June)
  • Lagos (1st of July - 6th of July)
  • Oyo (9th of July - 13th of July)
  • Osun (16th of July - 20th of July)

Criteria and How to Play

Buy at least 1 complete pack of SWAG Box for a household or an individual and 2 for each school.

Register on this website.

Play the qualifying rounds to qualify for the grand finale.

You can compete as an individual or as a school representative.

Why Sponsor SWAG?

SWAG - Swing Academic Game offers a unique opportunity for businesses and organizations to align themselves with the promotion of academic excellence and youth development. By sponsoring SWAG, you are investing in the future generation of leaders and innovators while gaining valuable exposure and recognition.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Title Sponsor

As the Title Sponsor of SWAG, your brand will be prominently featured in all promotional materials, including event signage, marketing collateral, and online platforms. This sponsorship level offers maximum exposure and recognition as the primary supporter of SWAG.

Gold Sponsor

Gold Sponsors receive prominent recognition on SWAG's website, social media channels, and event materials. This sponsorship level provides excellent visibility and demonstrates your commitment to supporting educational initiatives and youth development.

Silver Sponsor

Silver Sponsors enjoy recognition on SWAG's website and selected marketing materials. This sponsorship level offers exposure to our audience of students, educators, and parents, showcasing your dedication to fostering academic excellence.

Bronze Sponsor

Bronze Sponsors receive recognition on SWAG's website as well as selected promotional materials. This sponsorship level provides an opportunity to show your support for SWAG's mission and impact the lives of students across various states.
