Training on Game-Based Learning (GBL) for Early Career Educators and Teachers

Join us for a comprehensive Training on Game-Based Learning (GBL) designed specifically for early career educators and teachers in Ekiti State. Game-Based Learning is an innovative approach that uses games to engage students, enhance learning outcomes, and promote critical thinking skills.

Details

Date: Saturday, 18th of May, 2024

Time:12noon - 3pm

Venue: 7, Tunji Osunnuyi Street, behind Dave Hotel, Adehun, Ado Ekiti.

Space Limit: Only 30 spots available.

Benefits of the Training

  • Training Materials:Gain access to valuable training materials and resources to support your implementation of GBL in the classroom

  • Certificate:Receive a certificate of completion upon finishing the training, demonstrating your commitment to professional development

  • Refreshment:Enjoy complimentary refreshments during the training session.

  • Future Training and Job Opportunities:Stay informed about future training sessions and job opportunities in the field of education..

Who Should Attend

This training is ideal for:

  • Early career educators
  • Teachers looking to incorporate innovative teaching methods
  • Educators interested in enhancing student engagement and learning outcomes

Note: Attendance is free but registration is compulsory!
