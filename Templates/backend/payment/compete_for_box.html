How it works

The "Compete for SwagBox" option allows students who are unable to afford the full price of the SwagBox, priced at ₦25,000, to participate by paying a reduced fee of ₦2,500. To be eligible, 20 students from the same school must collectively pay the ₦2,500 fee. Once this condition is met, all participating students become eligible to compete for the SwagBox. The winner of the competition gets to use the SwagBox like any other participant in the championship.
